# Sales Templates & Objection Handling

## Founder Sales Messages

### Template A: Warm (JTBD interview contacts)
```
Hi [Name], quick update - remember when you told me about [their exact words about the problem]?

I built it. [One sentence - what it does + one specific result].

I'm keeping the first 10 users at $[price]/month as founding members for 12 months (going to $[higher price] when I open up). You'd also get a direct line to me to shape the product.

Want me to set up your account this week? I can walk you through it in 10 minutes.
```

### Template B: Waitlist / fake door signups
```
Hi [Name], you signed up for [product] a few weeks ago. It's ready.

[One sentence key benefit, using JTBD language].

I'm looking for 10 founding members at $[price]/month (going to $[higher] soon). Would a quick 10-min walkthrough make sense?
```

### Template C: Cold outreach (Reddit/LinkedIn)
```
Hi [Name], your [post/comment] about [problem] caught my attention - I just built a tool that [specific outcome, not feature].

[One sentence social proof or result].

I'm looking for 10 founding users at $[price]/month to help shape it. Would a quick 10-min demo make sense?
```

**Never say:** "No pressure", "Happy to answer questions", "Would you be open to..."

## Follow-Up Sequence

**Day 3 (no response):**
```
Hey [Name], just bumping this up - did you get a chance to look at this?
```

**Day 7 (share value):**
```
Hey [Name], quick update - [another user/you personally] just used it for [specific result]. Thought you'd find that interesting given what you told me about [their problem].
```

**Day 14 (breakup):**
```
Hey [Name], I've reached out a few times and haven't heard back - totally understand if the timing isn't right. I'll stop following up, but the founding member offer is open anytime. Just reply whenever.
```

**Key stat:** 44% of people give up after one message. 80% of sales happen after the 5th contact.

## Objection Handling

| They say | You say |
|----------|---------|
| "It's too expensive" | "Compared to what?" (then listen - they reveal their reference point) |
| "I need to think about it" | "Usually that means something specific isn't clicking. Is it the price, the timing, or you're not sure it'll solve [problem]?" |
| "Can I try it for free?" | "Try it for a week at full price. If it doesn't deliver [outcome], full refund. I take all the risk." |
| "I don't have budget" | "When does your budget cycle reset?" (qualify - if no money, move on) |
| "Send me more info" | "Sure - what specifically would be most helpful?" (prevents the info graveyard) |

## Design Partner Agreement (for founders not ready to charge directly)
```
I'm looking for 3-5 design partners to shape [product].

You get: Full access for 60 days + direct line to me for feature requests + founding member pricing ($[price]/mo) locked in when the trial ends.

I ask: 15-min weekly feedback call + honest input + commitment to start paying $[price]/mo on [date].
```
